Garlic Noodles Recipe from Thailand

Ingredients

  • Oyster Sauce
  • Angel hair pasta
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Add the oyster sauce, brown sugar, soy sauce and sesame oil to a bowl and stir until combined
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the noodles
  3. While the pasta cooks, mince the garlic and slice the green onions.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and enjoy
